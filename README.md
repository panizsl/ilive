## 🛠 Tech Stack

*   **Core:** Next.js 16 (React 19)
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4, Class Variance Authority (CVA), clsx, tailwind-merge
*   **Data Fetching:** Apollo Client (GraphQL)
*   **Animations:** Framer Motion
*   **Icons & Hooks:** Lucide React, Mantine Hooks

---

## 🗺 Available Pages & Routes

Currently, navigation is structured via direct URLs. To view specific pages during local development (`http://localhost:3000`), use the following routes:

**Main Pages:**
* `/aboutus` - About Us
* `/contactus` - Contact Us
* `/privacypolicy` - Privacy Policy
* `/pricing` - Pricing & Plans
* `/helpcenter` - Help Center
* `/downloadcenter` - Download Center
* `/casestudies` - Case Studies (eSports & Streaming)

**Dynamic & Section Pages:**
* `/bloglanding` - Blog Overview
* `/developer` - Developer Resources
* `/tutorials` - Video Tutorials
* `/services` & `/services/[slug]` - Services & Details
* `/products/[slug]` - Product Details
* `/integrations` & `/integrations/[slug]` - Software Integrations (OBS, vMix, etc.)
* `/jobopportunities` & `/jobopportunities/[slug]` - Careers

**Solutions:**
* `/solutions/users`
* `/solutions/applicants`
* `/solutions/industries`
* `/solutions/[slug]`
